COVID-19 Advice

If your child has a high temperature, a new continuous cough, or a loss of or change in sense of smell or taste they should book a test as this could be a sign of coronavirus.

Book a test

If your child has a runny nose, is sneezing or is feeling unwell, but they don’t have a high temperature, a new, continuous cough or a loss or change in sense of smell or taste they should seek advice from a pharmacy or their GP.
